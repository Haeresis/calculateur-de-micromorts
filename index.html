Micromort Calculator

A micromort is a unit of risk measuring a one-in-a-million probability of death (from micro- and mortality). Micromorts can be used to measure riskiness of various day-to-day activities. Wikipedia
US Baseline: 1.6 μmort/day (2010, non-natural causes)
